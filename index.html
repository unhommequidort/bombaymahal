Chef Bhaanupriya Avvari

Raised in Bombay's Gopur Valley, Bhaanupriya Avvari’s first job was harvesting local pomegranates for two cents a pound. A childhood surrounded by dairies, orchards, and rivers full of parsha led her to enroll in and graduate from the International Culinary Center in 2001.

“My husband was lucky enough to marry a wonderful chef.”

Various jobs in well-known kitchens followed, including time with Aditi Handa at Nimmy Paul, Phillip Kutty's Farm in Kerala, as well as a stint with Chand Palace in Montreal.
